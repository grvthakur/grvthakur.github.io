About

Who Am I?

I'm a Data Engineer at Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) with over 5 years of experience designing and delivering scalable data infrastructure. I hold dual Databricks Certified Data Engineer credentials — Professional (Mar 2026) and Associate (Oct 2025) — and specialize in Apache Spark, PySpark, Delta Lake, and ETL/ELT pipeline architecture on cloud and on-premise platforms.

My work focuses on Medallion Architecture, Delta Live Tables, and Databricks Workflows — building reliable data systems that deliver high-quality data to downstream consumers at scale. Beyond the lakehouse, I've built strong foundations in MS SQL Server, T-SQL performance tuning, REST API ingestion, and data governance with Unity Catalog.

I graduated in Electrical & Electronics Engineering from Birla Institute of Technology, Ranchi, which taught me to think in systems — a mindset that translates well to data engineering.
